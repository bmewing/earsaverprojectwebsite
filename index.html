Ear Saver Project

We are a group of volunteers across the US including the Volunteer State working to equip front-line medical professionals with 3D printed resources to improve their quality of life during this unprecedented pandemic at no cost to them.

As of May 2020, we have delivered approximately $12,000 worth of ear savers to 100+ different hospitals and medical offices across 17 states!
